Privacy Policy

Last Updated July 15, 2012

Knowing what information you provide us and how we use it is important, and that's what we want you to know from this privacy policy. In a nutshell, we won't sell your information, but we do require you give us a couple pieces of information about yourself in order to provide you with our product, {{ config.SITE_NAME }}. And just so everyone understands, when we say words like service or product, we're referring to everything this website does for you.

Information We Collect

In order for us to provide you this service we have to collect various pieces of information. The amount of personal information you need to provide is extremely minimal and includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your Name (First and Last)
  • Your Email Address
  • Your Mobile Phone Number
  • Your Breeze® Card Number

Some of this information, such as your email address, is required to register with this service, while others, such as your first and last name, are not.

We will never require you to enter any highly confidential personal information such as your Social Security number, credit card number, or debit card number. {{ config.SITE_NAME }} is a free service and there is currently no need for us to gather this information from you.

Why We Collect This Information

Plain and simple, we only require you to give information that allows us to provide you with explicitly the services provided on this website including:

  • Monitoring Your Breeze® Card Details
  • Maintaining Your Personal Reminders
  • Maintaining Your Personal Details (Listed Above)

What We Do With Your Information

First and foremost, we will never or sell or share your information to any third party. It's bad karma. We also won't use your personal information outside of providing you the services on this website without your express permission. However, we do store your information on our servers for the purpose of providing our services to you without having to require you to continuously provide us with it. Also, and this is important, we will occasionally use your Breeze® Card card numbers to retrieve and store the details about your card we provide. This is done by contacting a third party service, but in no way do we ever send any personally identifiable information along with your Breeze® Card number to this service. This communication is the only outside communication and is done securely over SSL.

This site is sponsored using advertisements. And as such you should be aware that third party advertisers may be reading or setting cookies from your browser in order to deliver advertisements to you. Because of this, you should know that we do not store any personal information about you via cookies.

Cookies

Cookies are required to use this site. We use cookies expressly for keeping track if you are logged into the site or not. Note that, for your own security, we do not store any personally identifiable information about you via cookies. At some points, to deliver advertisements, some third party advertisers may read or set cookies on your browser. This is done in the course of serving advertisements to you. Also, some third party services we use to monitor site traffic may use cookies to collect completely anonymous site traffic data. At no point do we, nor any third party, explicitly monitor your browsing habits.

Maintaining Your Information

You can directly control the personal information we have about you just by logging into this website. Also, if at any point you delete stored information we have, we will delete it permanently from our system. On the same note, you can also delete your entire account permanently by contacting us and simply requesting that we remove your account.

Contacting Us

If at any point you want to contact us about this privacy policy or should you have any other questions please don't hesititate to contact us by emailing help@{{ config.DOMAIN }}
